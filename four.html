Our team will handle the setup and removal of our top-of-the-line outdoor movie theatre equipment. We currently serve the cities of Menlo Park, Atherton, Woodside, Redwood City, San Carlos, Palo Alto, Los Altos, and Portola Valley.


Cleanliness Procedures

Due to COVID-19, safety is our #1 priority, and our team is taking precautions to keep you and your guests safe. Our projectors, speakers, screens and other equipment are extensively cleaned with disinfectant before and after each use. Additionally, our team wears masks and gloves during setup and takedown. We also use a contactless digital payment system.
